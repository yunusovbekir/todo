About the application

Quite minimal app with various functionality.

I demonstrate my basic different skills on this app.

  • You can created your task and, if you wish, you can share your task by providing other users' username, and also you can give an access to write a comment on your task. You can delete all the comments on your task. You can update and delete all your comments on another users' task.
  • You set a deadline for the task, and you will receive a notification by email. Notifications sent asynchronously by Celery. The broker is Redis
  • Commenting feature has been built with Django-channels. Users can show their comments real-time. (UPDATE)
  • While the comment are stored on a MySQL database, all the other information is kept on a Postgres database. Django multi-database feature has been implemented.
